Why should you listen to me about skincare?

Great question! I am not a certified skin specialist nor did I go to any special schooling for dermatology. However, I was addicted to TikTok at a certain point in my life, and much of my feed included content from board-certified dermatologists and people who genuinely were qualified to give skincare advice. With this, feel free to look into the tips and products I suggest, but doing your own research will be important too. Everyone is different, and our skin can react to products differently than others. My skincare journey, similar to many others, has been filled with trial and error. So, I hope to help you avoid the obvious mistakes I made and possibly point you in the right direction to building a consistent and efficient skincare routine.
